# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to N3RD Trivia ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Data Collection

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name (when you create an account)
- **Game Data:** Scores, game statistics, achievements, progress
- **User Preferences:** Settings, theme preferences, accessibility options

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Game sessions, features used, time spent in app
- **Analytics Data:** App crashes, performance metrics, user interactions

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information:
- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage
- **RevenueCat:** Subscription management and payment processing

## 3. Use of Data

We use the collected data for:
- Providing and improving our services
- Processing subscriptions and payments
- Analyzing app usage and performance
- Sending important service updates
- Personalizing your gaming experience
- Ensuring app security and preventing fraud

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers:** Firebase, RevenueCat (for app functionality)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger, acquisition, or sale

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication via Firebase
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of certain data collection (analytics)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@n3rdtrivia.app

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Necessary to provide our services
- Required by law or for legal purposes

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings.
